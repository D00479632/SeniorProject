Mapping Cave Systems is a power book that can be obtained from a box in the back room of the Adventurer's Guild, which is accessible after killing 1,000 monsters.[1] It can also be purchased from the Bookseller for data-sort-value="20000">20,000g starting in Year 3 (≈9% chance to appear).[2]

The first reading grants the player a power that gives them a 50% discount on Marlon's Item Recovery Service. Once read, it can be found in the Player's Menu on the Special Items & Powers tab. Each subsequent reading gives the player 100 Combat XP.
