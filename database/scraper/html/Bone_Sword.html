The Bone Sword is a sword weapon that can be purchased from the Adventurer's Guild for data-sort-value="6000">6,000g after reaching floor 75 in The Mines. It also has a 4% chance to be dropped from Skeletons. It is a possible reward for the chest on floor 90 of the Mines if "remixed" mine rewards are selected in the Advanced Options menu when starting a new game.

History

  • 1.5: Skeletons now have a 4% chance of dropping a Bone Sword. Level lowered from 10 to 5 and sell price lowered from data-sort-value="500">500g to data-sort-value="250">250g.
