The Morel is a mushroom found in the Spring via foraging in the Secret Woods or on the Forest Farm. It can also be found in the Farm Cave after choosing the mushroom option, or produced by a Mushroom Log. One Morel may be purchased from George's shop at the Desert Festival for data-sort-value="30"> 30 Calico Eggs.

Tailoring

The Morel is used in the spool of the Sewing Machine to create the Morel Shirt. It can be used in dyeing, serving as an orange dye at the dye pots, located in Emily's and Haley's house, 2 Willow Lane.

Quests

The Morel is not used in any quests.
