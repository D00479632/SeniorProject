Desert Trader
Open Hours: Always
Closed: 15-17 Winter
Address: Calico Desert
Occupants: Desert Trader

The Desert Trader is a merchant located in the Calico Desert who sells a variety of exotic goods. The Desert Trader does not accept gold, but only barters in exchange for other items.

During the Night Market, the corner shop in the Desert is closed and no purchases are possible. Instead, the Desert Trader can be spoken to at the Night Market for one free cup of Coffee per night.

Notes

  • The Desert Trader does not accept gold, and will explain to the player "Your coin has no value where I come from.... Do you have anything else to trade?"
  • Sandy mentions the Desert Trader during Emily's visit on her birthday (Fall 15). If the player interacts with her while she is standing in front of the Desert Trader, Sandy says "I think she sells some rare products."
