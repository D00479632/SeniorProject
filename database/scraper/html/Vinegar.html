Vinegar is a cooking ingredient that can be used in a variety of recipes. It can be purchased for data-sort-value="200">200g at Pierre's General Store. It can also be purchased from George's shop at the Desert Festival for data-sort-value="5"> 5 Calico Eggs. Two Vinegar can also be made by putting Rice into a Keg.

When used on a tree, it prevents the tree from growing Moss.
