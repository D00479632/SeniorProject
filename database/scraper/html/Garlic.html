Garlic is a vegetable crop that grows from Garlic Seeds after 4 days. The seeds are available at Pierre's General Store starting in Year 2. One Garlic can sometimes be purchased for data-sort-value="2500">2,500g from Pierre's booth at the Feast of the Winter Star.

Tailoring

Garlic can be used in the spool of the Sewing Machine to create the dyeable Shirt And Belt.

Quests

  • A fully-grown and unharvested Garlic is required for Gourmand Frog's puzzle on Ginger Island.
